01 · Wellness

Spa &Wellness Centre

Surrender to a world of tranquility at our serene spa. Our treatments draw inspiration from ancient Ayurvedic traditions and modern therapeutic techniques, creating a holistic healing experience that restores mind, body, and spirit.

Set amidst the natural beauty of Jim Corbett, each treatment room is designed to be a sanctuary of calm, with natural materials, soft lighting, and the gentle sounds of nature completing your experience.

03 · Activities

Nature &Adventure Activities

Corbett Safari

Our team assists with booking Jeep and Canter safaris into Jim Corbett National Park, giving you the best chance of spotting tigers, leopards, and exotic wildlife.

Nature Walks

Guided nature walks through our orchard grounds and surrounding areas, discover local flora, breathe in the fragrance of litchi blossoms, and reconnect with the earth.

Bird Watching

Jim Corbett is a birder's paradise. Spot kingfishers, hornbills, peacocks, and over 600 species of birds with guidance from our experienced naturalists.

Indoor Games

Enjoy our recreation room with billiards, table tennis, board games, and more, perfect for family bonding or friendly competition on rainy evenings.

Outdoor Recreation

Our lush lawns host badminton, cricket nets, and open-air yoga. The swimming pool is perfect for a morning dip before breakfast.

Campfire Evenings

End your day around a warm campfire under the stars, sharing stories and savoring our signature beverages amidst the sounds of the forest.
