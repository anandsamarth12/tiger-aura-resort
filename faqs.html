Everything you need to know about your stay at Tiger Aura Resort

Accommodation

We offer four room categories: Executive Room (comfortable stay with essential amenities, up to 2 adults), Premium Room With Balcony (spacious room with a private balcony, up to 2 adults), Elite Room with Balcony (enhanced amenities with balcony views, up to 2 adults + 1 child), and Duplex Room With Jacuzzi (two-level suite with a private jacuzzi, ideal for couples and families up to 4 guests).

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability. Please inform us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

No. Breakfast is not complimentary with standard room tariffs for any room type. It is available at our restaurant at a nominal cost. Complimentary breakfast is included only when explicitly stated in a promotional offer or package at the time of booking.

Our Duplex Room With Jacuzzi can accommodate up to 4 guests making it ideal for families. Children above 5 years are welcome. Children below 5 stay complimentary when sharing with parents using existing bedding. We can arrange an extra cot on request.

We love animals but for the safety and comfort of all guests and due to our proximity to a wildlife zone, pets are currently not permitted inside the resort premises.

Dining

Our restaurant, The Forest Kitchen, serves a diverse menu including North Indian, South Indian, Pan-Asian, Continental, and Local Kumaoni cuisines. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients wherever possible.

Absolutely. We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and Jain meal options. Please inform us of any dietary requirements or allergies at the time of booking so our kitchen team can be fully prepared.

Breakfast is served from 7:00 AM – 10:30 AM, Lunch from 12:30 PM – 3:00 PM, and Dinner from 7:30 PM – 10:30 PM. Light snacks and beverages are available throughout the day.

Yes, room service is available from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.

Activities & Experiences

We offer a wide range of experiences including guided jungle safaris in Jim Corbett National Park, bird watching, nature walks, evening campfire, yoga sessions, spa treatments, a fully equipped gym, cycling, and cultural village tours.

The outdoor pool is operational from March to November. During the cooler winter months, access may be restricted based on weather conditions.

Yes, we coordinate jeep and canter safaris into Jim Corbett National Park. Safaris are subject to Forest Department availability and permit allocation. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak season (October–April).

Spa services are primarily offered to in-house guests. Day spa appointments may be available on request subject to slot availability. Please call ahead to check.

Location & Travel

We are located in Chhoi Village, Ramnagar, Uttarakhand, just a short drive from the main Jim Corbett National Park entry gates. The exact address is Chhoi Village, Ramnagar, Jim Corbett, Uttarakhand 244715. View exact location on Google Maps.

Delhi is approximately 260 km away (5–6 hours by road). Take NH9 via Moradabad towards Ramnagar, then continue to Chhoi Village and follow Google Maps to Tiger Aura Resort. Trains run from Delhi to Ramnagar Railway Station (approx. 6 hours; resort is about 6 km from the station). We provide complimentary pickup from Ramnagar station on request. Get directions on Google Maps.

Yes. The nearest airport is Pantnagar Airport (approximately 80 km). We can arrange transfers from Pantnagar, Haldwani, or Delhi at additional cost. Please inform us 48 hours in advance.

Yes, complimentary parking is available for all in-house guests within the resort premises.
