Booking Procedure

How toReserve Your Stay

To ensure a seamless reservation experience, all room bookings require an advance payment for confirmation.

01

Submit Enquiry

Fill out the booking enquiry form with your preferred dates and requirements.

02

Availability Confirmation

Our reservations team will confirm availability within 4–6 working hours.

03

Advance Payment

Pay the advance amount to block your rooms. Booking remains tentative until payment.

04

Booking Voucher

Receive your booking confirmation voucher via email upon payment confirmation.

Important Note

Rooms remain on a tentative basis until advance payment is received. Management reserves the right to release unconfirmed bookings without notice. All rates are subject to applicable taxes.
