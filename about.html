Our Story

Born from a Love forNature & Hospitality

Tiger Aura Resort was born from a deep passion for Jim Corbett's extraordinary wilderness and a belief that every traveler deserves an experience that is both transformative and luxurious.

Nestled in Chhoi Village, Ramnagar, our resort sits at the threshold of one of India's most celebrated national parks, offering guests an unparalleled opportunity to connect with nature without compromising on comfort.

From the lilting calls of exotic birds at dawn to the sweet fragrance of litchi blossoms that fills our evenings, every moment at Tiger Aura is a gentle reminder of why this corner of Uttarakhand is so special.
