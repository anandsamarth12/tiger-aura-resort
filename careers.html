Why Work atTiger Aura?

At Tiger Aura Resort, we believe our greatest asset is our people. We nurture a culture of excellence, empathy, and passion for hospitality, set against the stunning backdrop of Jim Corbett's wilderness.

Whether you're starting your hospitality journey or looking to advance your career, you'll find a welcoming environment where your contributions are genuinely valued.

Ready to JoinOur Family?

We are always looking for talented, passionate individuals to join our growing team. Whether you are an experienced professional or a fresh graduate with the right attitude, we'd love to hear from you.

How to Apply

  1. Send your updated CV and a brief cover letter to info@tigerauraresort.com
  2. Mention the position you are applying for in the subject line.
  3. Our HR team will review your application and reach out within 7 working days.
  4. Selected candidates will be invited for an interview at the resort or via video call.
